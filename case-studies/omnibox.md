# Omnibox
**Category:** Product Design — Employer: Code Koalas

---

## Case Study

Omnibox was an early internet TV product — a budget streaming service delivered as a set-top box at a time when TV over the internet was just finding its footing. As a Code Koalas client, the project required designing a UI that could hold up across a wide range of TV sizes and display quality, with no reliable baseline screen to design against.

Beyond the interface, the project extended into the full physical product experience: packaging design, developed in direct collaboration with the factory in China, including both the box and remote design. I designed the materials that went inside the packaging, and how the package itself assembled and communicated. I also developed the brand, the website, and ran user testing — and designed a car wrap for the brand, which was a first.
