# Sociy
**Category:** Product Design — Employer: Code Koalas

---

## Case Study

Code Koalas was a Drupal product development agency whose client roster included a large number of YMCAs across the country. The problem was familiar: the team kept solving the same structural and content problems for each one individually, with no shared foundation to build from.

I was tasked with normalizing the site structure across YMCA builds and designing a management layer on top — a CMS and web page management system that let YMCAs control their own classes, programs, and site content without engineering involvement. I also developed the Sociy brand and logo. The name draws from the Latin *socii*, meaning allies — the idea of many people within a community coming together to deliver for it.

The website template and dashboard system shipped to over 45 YMCAs nationally.

## Raw Notes (verbatim)

One of the agencies I worked at was called Code Koalas and they were a Drupal product development agency. A large number of their clients were YMCAs and what they saw was that they were repeatedly solving the same problems for YMCAs. I was tasked with normalizing the structure of the YMCA websites we were producing and building a dashboard for YMCAs to manage their classes, their programs, the content in their overall site. This is effectively a CMS and web page management system on top of their site. I also designed the logo. It was inspired by the meaning behind the word Sochi, which is Latin for allies. It's about many people in your station coming together to deliver for their community.

Oh and the website design and template system went out to over 45 YMCAs nationally.
